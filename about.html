Hello! I'm Taylor Baker

Taylor Baker is the founder and lead designer of Saxe Studio , a creative studio dedicated to bold, strategic, and story-driven design. With expertise in branding, web design, and marketing, Taylor helps businesses and creatives build identities that are authentic, impactful, and timeless. Her work blends visual storytelling with thoughtful design, ensuring every project connects with the right audience. Whether crafting a brand from the ground up or refining an existing identity, Taylor's goal is to create work that stands out, resonates, and inspires others.
